Cookie Void

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 1, 2026

This privacy policy applies to the Cookie Void app for iOS, developed by Zachary Foerst. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

Information We Collect

Cookie Void itself does not collect or store any personal information on our servers. The app does not require account creation or login of any kind.

The following third-party services are integrated and may collect data independently:

Google AdMob

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data for advertising purposes. On first launch, you will be asked for permission to track your activity for personalized ads via Apple's App Tracking Transparency prompt. You may decline and non-personalized ads will be shown instead.

Google Privacy Policy →

Apple Game Center

We use Apple Game Center for leaderboard functionality. Your Game Center display name and score are submitted to the global leaderboard. This data is governed by Apple's privacy policy.

Apple Privacy Policy →

Apple StoreKit (In-App Purchases)

Purchases are processed entirely by Apple. We do not receive, store, or have access to your payment information.

Data Storage

Your game progress — including cookies, upgrades, prestige level, achievements, daily bonus streak, and boost status — is stored locally on your device using iOS UserDefaults. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any server controlled by us.

Advertising

Cookie Void displays banner advertisements, rewarded video ads, and interstitial ads served by Google AdMob. Users who purchase the "Remove Ads" in-app purchase will no longer see banner advertisements. AdMob's data collection practices are governed by Google's privacy policy linked above.

Children's Privacy

Cookie Void is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us and we will take steps to delete such information.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
