Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start a focus session?

Open the app and tap the start button. The timer will begin counting down your focus session. Stay in the app to keep the timer running.

Can I customize the session length?

Yes — adjust your focus and break durations in the settings panel before starting a session.

Does the timer run in the background?

Ro uses local notifications to alert you when a session ends, even if the app is in the background.

My session history disappeared.

Session data is stored locally on your device. Deleting and reinstalling the app will erase your history.

How do I contact Apple about a billing issue?

Visit reportaproblem.apple.com for any billing or purchase disputes.
