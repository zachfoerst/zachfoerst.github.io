Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 1, 2026

This privacy policy applies to the Ro app for iOS, developed by Zachary Foerst.

Information We Collect

Ro does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers. The app does not require account creation or login.

All data generated by Ro — including session history, timer settings, and preferences — is stored locally on your device and never leaves it.

Notifications

Ro may request permission to send local notifications to alert you when a focus session or break ends. These notifications are generated entirely on your device and do not involve any external servers or data collection.

Children's Privacy

Ro is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the date at the top of this page.
